Returns and Exchanges

Our Policy

At BahBooks, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you can return or exchange the items under the following conditions:

  • You can return the product within 30 days of purchase.
  • The product must be in its original condition and packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns and exchanges.

How to Initiate a Return

Follow these steps to return or exchange your item:

  1. Contact our customer service team via email at returns@bahbooks.com.
  2. Provide your order details and the reason for the return or exchange.
  3. Ship the product back to the address provided by our team.

Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the returned item is received and inspected. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.
